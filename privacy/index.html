Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-25
App: Party Scout (app.partyscout)
Contact: vasiliy.vazhesov@gmail.com

Party Scout is a weekly events digest. We built it to be quiet and private: there is no account, no sign-up, no ads, and no advertising or analytics trackers.

What the app does with data

Event content. The app downloads the weekly event listings from https://party-scout.app. These are public requests; we do not attach any identifier to them. As with any web request, our host receives standard technical metadata (such as your IP address and device user-agent) to deliver the files. We do not use this to identify or track you.

Approximate location (city auto-detect). The first time you open the app — and only if you haven't already chosen a city — the app makes a request to one of two third-party IP-geolocation services, ipapi.co and ipwho.is, to guess your nearest city (San Francisco or Los Angeles). These services receive your IP address and return an approximate location. The app uses it once to pick a city and does not store it. The app does not request GPS / precise-location permission. You can override the choice at any time with the city picker.

  • ipapi.co privacy policy: https://ipapi.co/privacy/
  • ipwho.is: https://ipwho.is/

Your city choice is saved locally on your device (browser local storage) so the app remembers it next time. It never leaves your device and is removed if you clear app data or uninstall.

Outbound links. Event cards link to third-party sites (e.g. Resident Advisor, Eventbrite, venue pages, Google Maps). Once you follow a link, that site's own privacy policy applies.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect, store, or sell personal information.
  • We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not create user profiles or require any login.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Permissions

The app requests only Internet access (to download listings). No location, contacts, storage, camera, or other sensitive permissions.

Data safety summary

  • Data collected: None stored by the developer.
  • Data shared: Your IP address is sent to ipapi.co / ipwho.is for the one-time city auto-detect (approximate location, app functionality). No other data is shared.
  • Encryption in transit: Yes (HTTPS).
  • Account / data deletion: No account; clearing app data removes the only stored value (your city choice).

Changes
