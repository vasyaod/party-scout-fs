Pier Party at the Wharf: Careless Whisper + Kendo the DJ

Sat · 2026-07-11 · 2–6pm — Crab Wheel Plaza (Fisherman's Wharf), SF — Free

Free open-air block party on the Wharf — dancing on Jefferson St with a craft-cocktail bar and bistro seating steps from the bay

Pier Party at the Wharf brings a free four-hour afternoon block party to Jefferson Street at Fisherman's Wharf, Saturday July 11, 2–6pm. Kendo the DJ spins throwbacks with a karaoke intermission (2–3:30pm) before 80s tribute band Careless Whisper closes it out (4:30–6pm), alongside a craft-cocktail bar, bistro seating and open-air dancing. Free, all ages.
